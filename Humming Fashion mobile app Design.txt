

---------MOBILE APPLICATION-FIGMA DESIGN--------

Humming Fashion is a sleek and modern e-commerce mobile app design created in Figma. This design focuses on providing a seamless and engaging shopping experience, allowing users to explore, browse, and purchase fashion items with ease. The interface is crafted with a user-friendly approach to ensure smooth navigation and accessibility.

Design Features:
- Elegant UI with a visually appealing and intuitive design.
- Home Page showcasing trending products, categories, and recommendations.
- Product Listings with high-quality images and essential details.
- Product Details Page providing multiple images, descriptions, and pricing.
- Shopping Cart & Checkout for simple and efficient cart management.
- User Profile & Wishlist for personalized user accounts.
- Dark & Light Mode support for user preference.
- Mobile-Optimized layout designed specifically for mobile devices.
